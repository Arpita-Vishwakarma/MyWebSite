THIS IS MY STORY

I hail from Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, famously known as the "City of Lakes." My parents have dedicated themselves wholeheartedly to supporting my career journey, and as the first female in my family to graduate in engineering, I am driven to use my platform to share resources and break stereotypes. My mission is to inspire first-generation immigrants, people of color, working moms, and individuals from underprivileged backgrounds by being a living testament to the power of perseverance and education.

I completed my higher secondary education in 2020 with a focus on Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM) at Shri Sathya Sai Higher Secondary School in Bhopal. Following my schooling, I pursued a Bachelor's degree in Technology with a specialization in Computer Science and Engineering from the NRI Institute of Information Science and Technology (NIIST), graduating in 2024.

I am dedicated to the mission of democratizing knowledge, with a passion for empowering individuals to reach their highest potential. I strive to make learning accessible and impactful, creating opportunities for growth and development.

Outside of my professional work, I value spending quality time with my family and friends, who provide constant inspiration and joy. I enjoy watching movies, listening to music, and staying active through cycling and working out, a pursuit that I am quite passionate about. My commitment to fitness fuels my drive and enhances my productivity, as I believe a healthy body supports a healthy mind.

Content creation is another area where I find immense satisfaction, as it enables me to share valuable insights and experiences with a broader audience. By crafting meaningful content, I aim to inspire and engage, fostering a community of learners and thinkers.
